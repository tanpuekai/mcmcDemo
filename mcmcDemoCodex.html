Now Notice This

In a bimodal landscape, local proposals usually stay inside one hill. Long residence times are not always wrong, but they can be a sign that the chain has poor global exploration.

Metropolis Acceptance Rule

alpha = min(1, pi(x') / pi(x))

Symmetric proposals cancel out, so the target density ratio decides whether the move survives.
