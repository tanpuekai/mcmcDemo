About

Metropolis-Hastings

A random walk algorithm that proposes new states and accepts/rejects them based on the target distribution ratio.

α = min(1, π(x')/π(x) · q(x|x')/q(x'|x))

Gibbs Sampling

Sequentially samples from conditional distributions. Each variable is updated given the current values of all others.

xᵢ⁽ᵗ⁺¹⁾ ~ p(xᵢ | x₁⁽ᵗ⁺¹⁾,...,xᵢ₋₁⁽ᵗ⁺¹⁾,xᵢ₊₁⁽ᵗ⁾,...)

Sequential Monte Carlo

Maintains a population of particles that evolve, are weighted by likelihood, and resampled according to weights.

wᵢ ∝ p(y|xᵢ), Resample if ESS < threshold
